  • About Us

    ATS Construction Inc. is a small business contractor that has been in business for over 20 years. Serving San Fernando Valley, Los Angeles and Orange County areas. ATS specializes in residential and commercial building, remodeling and repairs of any nature. ATS works with various property management companies and currently maintenance over 400 commercial and residential buildings.

    Job quality, low pricing and word of mouth is what keeps us in business. As we know that everybody is very busy these days ATS can promise you that our company will always value your time. Nothing is a waste of time if you use the experience wisely.
